Welcome to the Parent Center! 🍼

Your ultimate shopping manual for infant care, secure accounts, and delivery trackers.

At BabyShopHub, we understand that parenting is a beautiful yet demanding adventure. Our mobile application is crafted to simplify your shopping experience by keeping baby food, cotton clothes, toys, and premium diapers just a few taps away. This manual helps you make the most of our application services.

How to Shop in the App 🛒

Our marketplace makes it incredibly easy to find products, manage a cart, and complete orders safely:

1

Search & Filter

Use the query bar to search by product names (e.g. onesies) or select horizontal category tabs.

2

Add to Cart

Tap the "+" button on items to add them. Adjust quantities in the shopping cart panel anytime.

3

Checkout Securely

Input your shipping address, fill in credit card credentials, and click "Authorize Payment".

Delivery & Shipping Options 🚚

We pride ourselves on dispatching orders rapidly so your baby never runs out of essentials:

  • Standard Delivery (Free): Delivered within 2 to 3 business days. Free for all orders!
  • Real-time Tracking: Once your payment is approved, we automatically send an itemized bill receipt to your registered email containing delivery progress coordinates.

Returns & Refunds Policy ↩️

Your satisfaction is our primary concern. Review our baby-friendly return terms below:

Returns Window: You can return any unopened diapers, organic purées, clothing, or toys within 30 days of purchase for a complete refund.

To initiate a return, navigate to your Profile page, tap "Contact Support", and specify your Order Invoice ID. Our staff will reply within 24 hours.

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) 🔒

Protecting your account records is essential. We utilize advanced security challenges to prevent compromises:

  • MFA Toggling: Under your Profile page, toggle the "Multi-Factor Authentication" switch.
  • Login Security Challenges: Once enabled, logging in will automatically generate a 6-digit OTP verification code dispatched directly to your registered email inbox. You must enter this code to complete authentication.
  • New Location Warnings: If our security center detects a login attempt from a simulated new device browser or location (e.g. London, Tokyo), we immediately send an **Unknown Location Alert Email** containing security reset steps.

Frequently Asked Questions ❓

We support Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover cards through our secure gateway checkout process.

Once an order checkout completes and receipt emails are dispatched, please notify support within 30 minutes to adjust details before dispatch.

If a product (e.g. Wooden Stacking Toy) shows "Out of Stock", simply bookmark it or tap "Notify Me". The absolute second our admin updates the stock, we fire an automated email alert to your registered address!
